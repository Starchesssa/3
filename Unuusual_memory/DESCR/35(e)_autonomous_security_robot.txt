Title: Running Brains Robotics to Showcase GR100 Security Robot at Eurosatory 2024

Description:
Running Brains Robotics, a prominent figure in the landscape of autonomous robotics in France, is set to make waves once again with its latest innovation. The company has announced its participation at Eurosatory 2024, where it will introduce the world to its groundbreaking creation, the GR100 autonomous security robot. Scheduled to take place at the Parc des Expositions de Paris Nord Villepinte from June 17 to 21, 2024, Eurosatory provides the perfect platform for Running Brains Robotics to showcase its cutting-edge technology to a global audience.
