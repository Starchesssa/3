Title: How to make Smart trash bin using Arduino

Description:
In this project, I will show you How to Make a Smart Dustbin using Arduino, where the lid of the dustbin will automatically open when you approach with trash. The other important components used to make this Smart dustbin are HC-04 Ultrasonic Sensor and SG90 TowerPro Servo Motor.
