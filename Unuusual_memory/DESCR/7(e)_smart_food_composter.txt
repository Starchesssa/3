Title: Compost Connect: How Does It Work?

Description:
Chef Neil Perry and Compost Connect's executive director, Gary Smith, clear up some misconceptions on composting and show how easy it is for businesses to get started through Compost Connect. More than being just good for the environment, it's good for business, too. Tune in to see how it works.
