Title: GIO Pillows - music playing pillows with inbuilt speakers and Bluetooth connectivity

Description:
GIO Clavis Co, Ltd., is from Korea (established in 2011) and specialises in pillows, earlier baby pillows. Here they show their smart pillow which features two small speakers inside for night time listening. The speakers cannot be felt by hand so there is no chance of injury to the head from hitting the pillow too hard, though harsh use is not advised. The pillow is also washable, and comes with an external optional controller for music track playback control.
