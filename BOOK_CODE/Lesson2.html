KEY LESSON:

YOUR BIGGEST PROBLEM CAN BECOME YOUR BIGGEST PRODUCT.

Fast forward to 2003. Amazon had survived the dot-com bust. But inside, it faced a tangled web of messy technology.

Different teams had built different parts. Nothing worked well together — like building a skyscraper on sand.

Rebuild

Engineers rebuilt everything from the ground up — creating standardized “LEGO bricks” for all developers.

AWS

Then came the billion-dollar idea. They realized this internal solution could power every company in the world.

By 2022, AWS was generating $80B in revenue. A side project became Amazon’s most profitable division.

Lesson:

The solution you build for yourself might be the next big product the world is waiting for.
